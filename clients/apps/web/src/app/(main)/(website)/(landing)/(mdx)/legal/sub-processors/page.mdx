To support Polar in delivering its services, Polar engages third-party service providers and affiliates to support the delivery and operation of the Polar platform.

**What is a Sub-processor?**
Polar’s third-party service providers and affiliates, referred to as **Sub-processors** under the General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”), may process personal data on behalf of Polar and its merchants.

Sub-processors are service providers that may access or process personal data on behalf of Polar in order to provide infrastructure, operational support, or other services necessary for Polar to deliver its platform.

This page provides information about the Sub-processors and other Service Providers Polar uses, including their locations and a description of the services they perform.

**Due Diligence**

We perform due diligence including a security assessment on all of our service providers, including sub-processors. We also ensure that our contractual terms require that they process personal data only for the purposes of providing services to Polar and in accordance with applicable data protection laws and our terms.
