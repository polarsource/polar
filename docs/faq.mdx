  <Accordion title="Does my organization name need to be a registered company name?">
    No. Your organization name does not need to be your registered company name. It's the name shown to your customers on:

    - **Checkout pages** — the name customers see when they purchase your products
    - **Emails** — the sender name on all transactional emails customers receive
    - **Bank statement descriptors** — what appears on your customers' bank statements

    If you're an individual creator, you can use your personal name or any brand name you'd like to be known by. No registered company is required.
  </Accordion>
