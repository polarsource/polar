A single price doesn’t work for a global audience. $49/mo might be reasonable in the US, but it could represent days of work in other countries. Parity pricing consistently drives 20-30% more international sales by showing location-based discounts to visitors from lower-income regions.

[Evendeals](https://www.evendeals.com/) is a purchase parity pricing platform that helps you adjust prices based on your customers’ location. Combined with Polar’s payment processing and tax compliance, you get a complete global sales stack.


## How It Works

Evendeals detects your visitor's country, shows a discount banner with a region-appropriate price, and automatically creates and manages discount codes in Polar. When a visitor applies the code at checkout, they pay the adjusted price through Polar's standard checkout flow.

**Key features:**

* **Automatic discount sync** - Discount codes are created and managed in Polar automatically. No manual coupon work.
* **Code rotation** - Codes rotate by time or view count. Leaked coupons expire automatically.
* **VPN detection** - Block discount abuse from users masking their real location.

## Setup Guide

### Sign up to Evendeals

Go to [evendeals.com](https://www.evendeals.com/) and create an account.

### Create a new deal and select Polar

In your Evendeals dashboard, create a new deal and select Polar as your payment provider.
<img src="/assets/features/integrations/evendeals/create-deal.png" />

### Connect Polar via OAuth

You'll be redirected to Polar's secure authorization page to grant access. The connection is instant and webhooks are set up automatically.
<img src="/assets/features/integrations/evendeals/polar-oauth.png" />

### Fill out your deal details

Choose products from your Polar account, set your deal name, and configure basic settings.
<img src="/assets/features/integrations/evendeals/fill-out-deal.png" />

### Configure country discounts

Set percentage or fixed discounts for each country group. Coupons sync instantly to Polar.
<img src="/assets/features/integrations/evendeals/country-discounts.png" />

### Add the script to your site

Copy the script tag and add it to your website. Done! Your visitors will now see a discount banner based on their region.

<img src="/assets/features/integrations/evendeals/add-to-site.png" />
