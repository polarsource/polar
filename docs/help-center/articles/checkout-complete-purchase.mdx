<Info>
**Audience:** Buyer — end customers completing a purchase at checkout or in the customer portal.
</Info>

Open the checkout link the seller shared. Enter your email and payment details on the secure checkout page. Submit the form to pay. After a successful payment, you will see a confirmation and any next steps (for example access to the customer portal or delivery of benefits).

If checkout fails, check your card details and try again, or use another payment method if offered. For tax or address errors, correct the fields shown and resubmit.
