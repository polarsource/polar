sidebarTitle: "Orders and invoices"
description: "Find past orders and invoices as a customer in the customer portal."
audience: buyer
---

<Info>
**Audience:** Buyer — end customers using checkout or the customer portal.
</Info>

Open the **customer portal** from the seller’s link and sign in if asked. Go to **Orders** (or similar) to see purchases. Open an order to view details and, if the seller enables it, download an invoice or receipt.

If a document is missing, contact the seller—they control what appears in the portal.
