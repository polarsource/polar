---
title: "How do I retry my subscription from the customer portal?"
sidebarTitle: "Retry subscription from portal"
description: "Retry a failed subscription payment or resume billing from the customer portal as a buyer."
audience: buyer
---

<Info>
**Audience:** Buyer — end customers using checkout or the customer portal.
</Info>

Open the **customer portal** from the link the seller sent you and sign in if prompted. Go to **Subscriptions** (or the equivalent section) and find the subscription that failed or needs payment.

If a payment failed, update your payment method if the portal allows it, then use **Retry** or **Pay now** (wording may vary) to charge again. If the subscription was paused or past due, completing the payment or confirming the update usually puts it back on track.

If you do not see a retry or pay option, contact the seller—their portal settings control which actions you can take.
