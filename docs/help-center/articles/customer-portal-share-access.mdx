sidebarTitle: "Share or revoke portal access"

audience: merchant
---

<Info>
**Audience:** Merchant — Polar sellers using the dashboard or APIs.
</Info>

You share the customer portal by sending customers a **customer portal session** or the hosted portal URL your app generates, depending on your integration. See [Customer portal](/features/customer-portal) for how sessions work and how to embed or link the portal.

To revoke access, invalidate or stop issuing new portal sessions for that customer and rotate any long-lived links you have shared. Use your dashboard or API to manage customer access according to your product’s flows.
