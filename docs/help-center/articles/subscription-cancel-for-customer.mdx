sidebarTitle: "Cancel subscription for a customer"

---

<Info>
**Audience:** Merchant — Polar sellers using the dashboard or APIs.
</Info>

Open your Polar dashboard and go to the customer or subscription you need to cancel. From the subscription detail view, use the cancel flow for that subscription. The exact steps depend on your product setup; you can typically cancel immediately or at the end of the current period depending on what you offer.

If you use the API, you can revoke or update subscriptions programmatically—see the [Subscriptions API](/api-reference/subscriptions/get) and related endpoints for your integration.
