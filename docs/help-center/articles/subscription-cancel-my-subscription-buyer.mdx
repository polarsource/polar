---
title: "How do I cancel my own subscription?"
sidebarTitle: "Cancel my subscription"
description: "Cancel your subscription as a customer when the seller allows it."
audience: buyer
---

<Info>
**Audience:** Buyer — end customers using checkout or the customer portal.
</Info>

Sign in to the **customer portal** with the link from the seller. Open the subscription you want to stop and look for **Cancel** or **Turn off renewal**. Some sellers only allow cancellation at the end of the billing period; others may stop access immediately.

If you do not see a cancel option, the seller may require you to contact them directly or may manage cancellations only from their side.
