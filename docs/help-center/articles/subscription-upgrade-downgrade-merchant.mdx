<Info>
**Audience:** Merchant — Polar sellers using the dashboard or APIs.
</Info>

Open the subscription in your Polar dashboard and use the available actions to move the customer to a different product or price. Depending on your setup, upgrades and downgrades may create proration charges or credits on the next invoice.

For automated flows, use the [Subscriptions API](/api-reference/subscriptions/update) so your app can switch plans when the customer picks a new tier. See also [Subscription upgrades](/guides/subscription-upgrades) and [Subscription downgrades](/guides/subscription-downgrades) for product behavior.
