[Refgrow](https://refgrow.com) is an affiliate and referral tracking platform that integrates with Polar to automatically track conversions, calculate commissions, and manage affiliate payouts.

<Info>
  This guide assumes you already have a Polar account with products or subscriptions set up.
</Info>

## What You Get

- Automatic tracking of purchases and subscriptions made through affiliate referral links
- Commission calculation (percentage or flat) with support for recurring commissions
- Affiliate portal where your partners can see their stats, earnings, and referral links
- Embeddable widget for your website or app
- Coupon-based attribution as an alternative to referral links

## Setup

<Steps>

### Create a Refgrow account

Sign up at [refgrow.com](https://refgrow.com) and create a new program. Set your commission structure (e.g., 20% recurring) and configure your program settings.

### Connect Polar

1. In Refgrow, go to **Integration** (Setup section in the sidebar)
2. Expand the **Polar** section
3. In Polar, go to **Settings → Access Tokens** and create a new access token
4. Copy the token and paste it into the Polar field in Refgrow
5. Click **Connect**

The webhook endpoint is created automatically — no manual webhook configuration needed. Refgrow registers a webhook with Polar that listens for subscription and order events.

### Add the tracking script

Add the Refgrow tracking script to your marketing site or landing pages. This captures referral clicks when visitors arrive via an affiliate link:

```html
<script
  src="https://scripts.refgrowcdn.com/latest.js"
  data-project-id="YOUR_PROJECT_ID"
  async
  defer
></script>
```

Replace `YOUR_PROJECT_ID` with your actual project ID from the Integration page in Refgrow.

### Invite affiliates

You can invite affiliates in two ways:

- **Manually**: Go to **Affiliates** in Refgrow and click **Add Affiliate** — they'll receive an email invitation with their referral link
- **Self-signup**: Enable the affiliate portal and share your portal link (e.g., `yourprogram.refgrow.com`) — affiliates can sign up on their own

</Steps>

## How It Works

1. An affiliate shares their unique referral link (e.g., `yoursite.com?ref=ABCDEF`)
2. A visitor clicks the link — the tracking script sets a cookie to remember the referral
3. The visitor subscribes or purchases through Polar
4. Polar sends a webhook to Refgrow with the payment details
5. Refgrow matches the customer to the referring affiliate
6. Commission is calculated and credited to the affiliate

### Attribution Methods

Refgrow supports multiple ways to attribute a sale to an affiliate:

- **Referral link cookie**: Set by the tracking script when a visitor clicks a referral link
- **Coupon code**: Assign unique coupon codes to affiliates — when a customer uses one at checkout, the sale is attributed automatically
- **Subscription metadata**: Pass `referral_code` in your Polar checkout metadata for server-side attribution
- **Email matching**: If the customer's email was previously captured as a referral, the sale is matched

## Embed the Affiliate Widget (Optional)

For SaaS products, you can embed the affiliate dashboard directly inside your app so users can become affiliates without leaving your product:

```html
<div
  id="refgrow"
  data-project-id="YOUR_PROJECT_ID"
  data-project-email="{{user.email}}"
>
</div>
<script src="https://scripts.refgrowcdn.com/page.js" async defer></script>
```

Replace `{{user.email}}` with the logged-in user's email from your auth system. This shows the user their affiliate dashboard with referral link, stats, and earnings — no extra login needed.
